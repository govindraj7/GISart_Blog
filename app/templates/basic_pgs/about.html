Users can upload and share satellite, remote sensing, or cartographic photos on this website. The USGS's photographic posts on Unsplash served as the site's primary source of inspiration. See a few of their pictures on this page.

Photo by USGS on Unsplash

A geographic information system is a type of database that combines software tools for managing, analysing, and displaying geographic data with the database itself. GIS connects data to a map and combines all kinds of descriptive data with location data. This offers a basis for mapping and analysis, which are applied in science and practically every sector of the economy. These visualisations can be artistic as well as analytical and functional.
